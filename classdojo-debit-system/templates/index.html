About This System

This debit card system allows students to use their ClassDojo points as currency within the school. Students can:

  • Earn points through positive behavior and achievements
  • Use physical debit cards linked to their accounts
  • Make purchases at the school store, cafeteria, and other locations
  • Track their spending and balance history

Getting Started:

  1. Import student data from ClassDojo (CSV export)
  2. Assign physical card IDs to each student
  3. Start processing transactions at various school locations
  4. Monitor balances and transaction history
